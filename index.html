Track 1 — Prompt

Build an interactive project showcase for a university presentation. Show 6 cards with a title, one-line summary, and tags. Add tag filters at the top and a search box. When a card is clicked, show a detail view with more information. Use a clean, professional design.

The “Interactive Sandwich”

Do not just drop a widget onto a blank slide.
Frame it with purpose.

Setup — Tell your audience why they are interacting
Your Widget — The interactive component
Synthesis — What is the takeaway?

Interaction ≠ Understanding

A professional-looking interface does not guarantee accurate content.
Always verify the substance behind the design.

Good for

Low-stakes exploration, teaching aids, prototypes

Not for

Clinical decisions, legal advice, graded assessments

When to Use What

Canva Native Features

  • Polls & Q&A (Canva Live)
  • Quizzes
  • Forms & surveys
  • Standard templates

Canva Code (Vibe Coding)

  • Custom explorers
  • Variable simulators
  • Unique data showcases
  • Anything templates can’t do

Where to Go Next
