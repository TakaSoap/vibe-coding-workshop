What is “Vibe Coding”?

The term was coined by AI researcher Andrej Karpathy in early 2025. It became Collins Dictionary’s Word of the Year 2025.

Describe → Generate → Inspect → Refine

Instead of writing code yourself, you describe what you want in plain language. The AI writes the code, you check the result, and you tell it what to change.

The “Enthusiastic Intern” Rule

Canva Code has limited capabilities. Think of it as a helpful but inexperienced assistant — great with simple tasks, unreliable with complex ones.

Keep it small. Keep it useful. Keep it stable.

Simple card layout? Excellent. 3D animated neon simulator? The intern will panic.

Live Build: Step by Step

On the Canva homepage, click the Canva AI text box. Then click the Code button at the bottom.
Tip: do not just type and press Enter — that opens design mode, not code mode.

Track 1 — Prompt

Build an interactive project showcase for a university presentation. Show 6 cards with a title, one-line summary, and tags. Add tag filters at the top and a search box. When a card is clicked, show a detail view with more information. Use a clean, professional design.

Notice the structure: we specified the audience, the inputs (filters, search), the behavior (click for details), and the style (clean, professional).

Inspect & Refine

Scenario A — Fix something

“The filter button does not work. Please fix it. Keep everything else the same.”

Scenario B — Improve it

“This looks great. Make the text larger for a presentation, and change the colors to blue and grey.”

Then click “Use in a design” to embed it directly in your slide.

The “Interactive Sandwich”

Do not just drop a widget onto a blank slide. Frame it with purpose.

🍞 Setup — Tell your audience why they are interacting
🥩 Your Widget — The interactive component
🍞 Synthesis — What is the takeaway?

Interaction ≠ Understanding

A professional-looking interface does not guarantee accurate content. Always verify the substance behind the design.
