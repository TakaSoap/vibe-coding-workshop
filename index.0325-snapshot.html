Track 1 — Prompt

Build an interactive showcase for a university presentation. Show 6 cards featuring projects, research themes, teaching ideas, student work, or service initiatives. Each card should include a title, one-line summary, and tags. Add tag filters at the top and a search box. When a card is clicked, show a detail view with more information. Use a clean, professional design.

The “Interactive Sandwich” Mindset

Frame interaction with purpose.

Setup — Tell your audience why they are interacting
Your Widget — The interactive component
Synthesis — What is the takeaway?

Use the next block flexibly: refine your component, embed it if you are ready, or explore Canva AI further. The important thing is the mindset behind the slide.

Interaction ≠ Understanding

A professional-looking interface does not guarantee accurate content.
Always verify the substance behind the design.

Good for

Low-stakes exploration, teaching aids, prototypes

Not for

Clinical decisions, legal advice, graded assessments
