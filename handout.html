Project / Idea Showcase

Best for: Anyone who wants to present a portfolio of projects with clickable tags and filters.

Prompt Template

Build an interactive project showcase for a university presentation. Show 6 cards with a title, one-line summary, and tags. Add tag filters at the top and a search box. When a card is clicked, show a detail view with more information. Use a clean, professional design.

Dummy Content (paste this after the prompt, or replace with your own)

Use these 6 projects:

1. "Urban Heat Map" — Mapping city temperatures using sensor data. Tags: Science, GIS
2. "Legal Aid Chatbot" — AI assistant for basic legal questions. Tags: Law, AI
3. "Patient Triage Tool" — Symptom-based priority sorting for clinics. Tags: Medicine, AI
4. "Heritage Walk AR" — Augmented-reality walking tour of historic sites. Tags: Humanities, Design
5. "Budget Planner" — Interactive household budget calculator. Tags: Education, Finance
6. "Lab Notebook" — Digital lab journal with photo upload. Tags: Science, Education

Case / Timeline Explorer

Best for: Humanities, Law, Education, or anyone working with cases, policies, or historical arguments.

Prompt Template

Build an interactive timeline showing the evolution of data privacy legislation. Show 5 milestones in chronological order on a horizontal timeline. When a milestone is clicked, display a short description of that law and its key impact. Use a clean, professional design.

Dummy Content

Use these 5 milestones:

1. 1995 — EU Data Protection Directive: First major EU regulation on personal data. Required member states to protect privacy during data processing.

2. 1996 — HIPAA (USA): Health Insurance Portability Act. Set standards for protecting sensitive patient health information.

3. 2018 — GDPR (EU): General Data Protection Regulation. Gave individuals control over personal data. Major fines for violations. Global impact.

4. 2020 — CCPA (California): California Consumer Privacy Act. Gave California residents new rights over their personal information collected by businesses.

5. 2024 — EU AI Act: First comprehensive AI regulation. Classified AI systems by risk level. Set rules for transparency and accountability.

Concept / Variable Explorer

Best for: STEM, Medicine, or anyone working with numbers and variables that affect an outcome.

Prompt Template

Build an interactive compound interest calculator. Add three sliders: one for initial investment (1000 to 50000), one for annual interest rate (1% to 15%), and one for time period (1 to 30 years). Calculate and display the final amount. Show a simple bar chart or growth line that updates when sliders change. Use a clean, professional design.

Dummy Content

Use these default values and labels:

Title: "Compound Interest Explorer"
Subtitle: "See how small changes in rate or time create big differences"

Default values:
- Initial investment: $10,000
- Annual interest rate: 5%
- Time period: 20 years

Formula: Final = Principal × (1 + rate)^years

Show the final amount as a large number.
Show a bar chart comparing Year 1, Year 5, Year 10, Year 20, Year 30.

Prompt Cheat Sheet

Structure your prompts with these four elements:

Purpose

“for a university presentation”

Elements

“6 cards with title, summary, and tags”

Behavior

“when clicked, show a detail view”

Style

“clean, professional design”

Common Refinement Prompts

Fix a bug

“The filter button does not work. Please fix it. Keep everything else.”

Change style

“Make the text larger and change the colors to blue and grey.”

Add a feature

“Add a search box at the top that filters cards by title.”

Simplify

“Remove the animations. Keep the layout clean and simple.”
